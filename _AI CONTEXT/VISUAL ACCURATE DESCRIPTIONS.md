# HAMMY
Hammy is a small adventure hamster whose entire design is built around the feeling of momentum, celebration, encouragement, and joyful progress. His silhouette is intentionally compact, rounded, and irresistibly approachable, making him feel more like a beloved companion than a generic cartoon animal. His body is short and chubby with oversized cheeks, a soft rounded belly, tiny paws, and a large expressive head that amplifies every emotion. His fur pattern is one of his strongest recognition anchors: rich golden-orange fur wraps around the sides of his face, ears, back, arms, and outer body, while a broad stripe of warm cream fur runs vertically from the top of his forehead down through the center of his face and continues into his chest and belly. The transition between colors should feel soft and fluffy rather than sharply separated. His cheeks are plush and slightly puffed, reinforcing his optimistic personality. His eyes are oversized, glossy, and highly expressive, featuring large dark pupils surrounded by subtle highlights that make him appear permanently excited, supportive, and emotionally engaged. His tiny pink nose sits above a cheerful smiling mouth that is often open in delight, as though he has just witnessed a learner achieve something meaningful. His ears are large relative to his head, with warm peach-pink interiors framed by orange fur. Delicate whiskers extend outward from his cheeks, helping maintain his hamster identity without drifting into generic rodent territory. Around his neck sits his signature SideQuestHQ indigo scarf displaying the official logo prominently at the center, while on his back rests a recognizable indigo adventure backpack complete with rolled travel gear, straps, badges, charms, and small explorer accessories. The backpack is not decorative; it is one of the core visual anchors that immediately identifies Hammy as the mascot of progress, milestones, and forward movement. Every aspect of his design should communicate the feeling that even the smallest achievement deserves celebration and that every completed step matters.  

Hammy should never feel like a mouse, squirrel, chipmunk, teddy bear, or generic mascot hamster. He is specifically the embodiment of visible progress within the SideQuestHQ universe, representing achievement, momentum, streaks, milestones, encouragement, and positive reinforcement. His poses are energetic and emotionally expressive, frequently shown waving a flag, cheering, holding rewards, celebrating wins, carrying adventure gear, presenting milestones, offering encouragement, or enthusiastically supporting learners as they move forward. Even when standing still, his posture should feel lively and optimistic, as though he is always one encouraging word away from celebrating the next victory. His tiny paws are highly expressive and often function as visual storytelling tools, clutching stars, trophies, hearts, notebooks, progress markers, or celebratory props. His emotional range leans heavily toward excitement, pride, encouragement, joy, and optimism rather than calmness or deep contemplation. Looking at Hammy should create the feeling of receiving positive reinforcement after completing something difficult. He represents the emotional payoff of progress itself. While Kip guides the journey, NovaPaw explores possibilities, and Pando sustains focus, Hammy is the moment when effort becomes visible and momentum becomes tangible. He is the cheerful companion who reminds learners that mastery is built from countless tiny wins accumulated over time. His presence should immediately communicate the SideQuestHQ belief that meaningful achievement is not created through giant leaps but through small consistent steps that deserve recognition and celebration. Every illustration of Hammy should feel like another chapter in the same ongoing story of progress, continuity, and joyful forward motion.

Hammy's color system is built around a carefully balanced spectrum of warm golden-orange fur, soft cream highlights, rich indigo accessories, and subtle peach-pink accents that together create a feeling of celebration, momentum, warmth, and optimism. The dominant fur color is a vibrant but approachable golden-orange, ranging from approximately #F59E0B across the brightest sunlit regions to deeper amber-orange tones around #E88A08 and #D97706 within recessed fur groupings and shadowed contours. His orange fur should never feel flat; it should flow through extremely soft gradients that move from luminous honey-gold highlights into richer toasted-orange shadows, creating depth while preserving a plush toy-like softness. Running through the center of his forehead, face, muzzle, chest, belly, and lower body is a broad field of warm cream fur, transitioning between #FFF8EC, #FAF7F2, #F7EAD7, and #F0DFC8. These cream regions should appear naturally brighter than the surrounding orange fur and create immediate visual contrast that helps define his facial structure. The fur rendering throughout the character should remain highly diffused, with highlights softly blooming across rounded surfaces and shadows blending gently into neighboring colors rather than forming hard edges. His cheeks, muzzle, and belly should appear especially luminous, as though softly illuminated from within. His eyes are among the highest-contrast elements in the design, built from near-black pupils (#121212), charcoal outer rings (#2A2A2A), bright ivory catchlights (#FFFDF8), and subtle radial gradients that create a glossy, emotionally expressive appearance. His nose uses warm coral-pink tones ranging from #FF9A7A to #F0826B, while the interiors of his ears and paw pads shift through soft peach gradients around #FFD4B8, #FFC3A0, and #FFB089, reinforcing warmth and friendliness. The signature SideQuestHQ scarf and adventure backpack are rendered using the official indigo family, transitioning from deep structural indigo (#3730A3) within folds, seams, straps, and recessed areas into rich primary indigo (#4F46E5) across dominant visible surfaces, with subtle highlights reaching toward #6D4CFF and #7C6CFF where fabric catches ambient light. Metallic badges, stars, and celebratory accessories incorporate Momentum Orange (#F59E0B), occasionally accented by brighter golden tones around #FFB11A to emphasize achievement and progress. Overall illumination should feel atmospheric and uplifting rather than directional, as though Hammy exists inside a bright optimistic world where every accomplishment generates its own glow. No element should use pure black, pure white, cold grays, harsh shadows, or overly saturated neon colors. The entire palette should feel warm, energetic, encouraging, and emotionally rewarding, creating the impression of a tiny companion whose very presence turns progress into celebration and every milestone into something worth smiling about.
