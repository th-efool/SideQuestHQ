# SideQuestHQ Color System

## Purpose

The SideQuestHQ color system exists to communicate the emotional foundations of the product.

Colors are not decorative.

Each color represents a specific role within the learning experience and should be used intentionally across product, brand, marketing, illustrations, and future experiences.

The palette should remain consistent even as visual styles evolve.

---

## Primary Indigo — #4F46E5

Represents:

- curiosity
    
- imagination
    
- exploration
    
- learning
    
- possibility
    
- intellectual depth
    

This is the primary brand color.

When a user thinks about SideQuestHQ, this should be the color they associate with the product.

Use when communicating:

- discovery
    
- learning paths
    
- exploration
    
- identity
    
- navigation
    
- primary brand presence
    

Primary Indigo is the color of curiosity.

---

## Dark Indigo — #3730A3

Represents:

- trust
    
- structure
    
- stability
    
- continuity
    
- reliability
    
- confidence
    

Dark Indigo provides grounding.

It gives weight to information and creates a sense of permanence.

Use when communicating:

- hierarchy
    
- important information
    
- system structure
    
- reliability
    
- progression
    

Dark Indigo is the color of continuity.

---

## Momentum Orange — #F59E0B

Represents:

- progress
    
- momentum
    
- achievement
    
- completion
    
- forward movement
    
- positive reinforcement
    

Orange should signal significance.

Its role is not to dominate interfaces but to draw attention toward moments of progress and action.

Use when communicating:

- milestones
    
- celebrations
    
- calls-to-action
    
- achievements
    
- streaks
    
- success states
    

Momentum Orange is the color of progress.

---

## Soft Cream — #FAF7F2

Represents:

- warmth
    
- clarity
    
- comfort
    
- balance
    
- openness
    
- breathing room
    

Soft Cream prevents the brand from feeling cold, sterile, or overly technical.

It provides space for other elements to exist without visual fatigue.

Use when communicating:

- accessibility
    
- calmness
    
- readability
    
- openness
    
- comfort
    

Soft Cream is the color of emotional safety.

---

# System Relationships

The colors should work together as a narrative:

Curiosity → Structure → Progress

Primary Indigo creates interest.

Dark Indigo provides direction.

Momentum Orange rewards movement.

Soft Cream ensures the experience remains approachable.

No single color should attempt to communicate all meanings simultaneously.

Each color has a distinct responsibility within the system.

---

# Design Principles

The palette should feel:

- modern
    
- optimistic
    
- approachable
    
- thoughtful
    
- confident
    

The palette should not feel:

- corporate
    
- aggressive
    
- clinical
    
- overly playful
    
- luxury-focused
    
- productivity-obsessed
    

---

# Long-Term Rule

Future designs, illustrations, interfaces, campaigns, and brand assets should preserve the semantic meaning of these colors even if the visual style evolves over time.

The exact appearance of SideQuestHQ may change.

The meaning of the colors should not.
