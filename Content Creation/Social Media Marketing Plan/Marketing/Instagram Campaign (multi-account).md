# Instagram Campaign (multi-account)

## Goal

- Top-of-funnel discovery + retargeting to landing page

## Account network (plan)

- 1 official brand account (product updates, highlights, credibility)
- 5 creator-style persona accounts (different niches/voices)
- 5 faceless theme pages (quotes, study loops, “how to focus” clips)

## Personas (examples)

- “Exam Sprint” (students)
- “ADHD Study System” (attention angles)
- “Career Switch Learning” (learning to code, etc.)
- “Study With Me” (aesthetic + routines)
- “Learning Planner” (systems + templates)

## Content formats

- Reels: hooks + quick payoff + soft CTA
- Carousels: checklists + templates
- Stories: polls + Q&A + link sticker (official only)

## Posting cadence

- Official: 3–5 posts/week
- Persona/faceless: 1 post/day/account (batch scheduled)

## CTA + links

- Official uses link-in-bio + story links
- Persona pages: value-first, link only when appropriate; avoid fake claims/testimonials

## Tracking

- UTM per account (`utm_source=ig_persona1`, etc.)
