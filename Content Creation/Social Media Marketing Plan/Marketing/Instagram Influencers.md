# Instagram Influencers

## Who to target

- Micro-influencers (5k–50k) posting productivity/studying content
- Prefer creators who already share tools/templates

## Offer

- Free premium access
- Affiliate link (10–30% rev share) OR flat fee later
- “Early creator partner” badge + featured spot

## Pitch DM (template)

- 1 line personal hook (specific video)
- What the product does (1 sentence)
- Why their audience fits
- Offer + easy CTA (try it with 1 playlist)

## Tracking

- Unique link per creator (UTM)
- Track: views → clicks → signups → activation

## Content ideas for them

- “I turned a 6-hour playlist into 14 days”
- “How I study 20 min/day”
- “My learning streak system”
