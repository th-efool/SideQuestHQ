# Bots Workflow (Twitter/Threads)

## What the bot does

- Monitors posts about: “can’t focus”, “saved videos”, “study system”, “playlist”
- Replies with helpful advice + optional soft link

## Accounts/keywords to monitor

- Keywords: “study routine”, “ADHD productivity”, “YouTube playlist”, “can’t finish”
- Communities: productivity Twitter, student Twitter, indie hackers (careful)

## Reply templates

- 3 helpful variants per keyword cluster
- Link only in 1/3 replies; keep the rest value-only

## Safety checklist

- Rate limits
- Avoid repetitive spam
- Manual review queue for risky replies
- Kill switch
