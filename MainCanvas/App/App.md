# Bottom Nav = Highest Frequency Actions

I would strongly consider:

|Icon|Tab|
|---|---|
|▶️|Learn|
|📚|My Cohorts|
|🔍|Explore|
|👥|Community|
|☺️|Me|

Not Feed.

Not Social.

Not Dashboard.

---

## 1. Learn (Center / Most Important)

This is your actual product.

Not cohorts.

Not profiles.

Not chats.

The learning session.

When user opens app:

> "What should I do right now?"

That's literally your value prop.

This should be the default tab.

Think Duolingo lesson button.

Think Spotify play button.

Think "Continue".

---

## 2. My Cohorts

This is the user's world.

Contains:

- active cohorts
- progress
- completion %
- priorities
- schedule
- streaks

This is where metrics belong.

Not profile.

Metrics are learning state.

Not identity.

---

## 3. Explore

Discover:

- AI cohorts
- Psychology cohorts
- Language cohorts
- Trending cohorts
- Friends joined

This aligns directly with your "Curious Explorer" persona.

---

## 4. Community

Not "Social."

Social sounds generic.

Community sounds purpose-built.

Inside:

- cohort chats
- study rooms
- nudges
- accountability
- body doubling
- activity

This supports the cohort/community angle people immediately understand.

---

## 5. Me

Tiny profile tab.

Contains:

- profile
- achievements
- settings
- notifications
- account

That's it.

Do NOT waste a primary tab on profile.

Profile is rarely visited.
