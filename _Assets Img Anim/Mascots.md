# Mascots

This document defines the mascot system used across SideQuestHQ.

The mascots are not random characters. They exist to reinforce different emotional states that appear throughout the learning journey.

The primary purpose of the mascot system is:

- make the product feel more human
- create emotional continuity
- celebrate progress
- reduce intimidation
- make learning feel alive
- visually communicate product states without requiring text

---

# Mascot Hierarchy

## 1. Kip (Primary Mascot)

**Status:** Main Mascot

Kip is the face of SideQuestHQ.

When people think about SideQuestHQ, they should think about Kip.

All other mascots are secondary supporting characters.

Kip represents the core promise of the product:

> Continue where you left off.

Kip appears throughout the entire learning experience and acts as the user's constant companion.

---

When the user opens SideQuestHQ, Kip represents the next step already waiting for them.

The others represent emotional states that appear throughout the learning journey.

### Learning Flow

NovaPaw
↓
"I found something interesting."

Kip
↓
"Here's your path."

Pando
↓
"Let's focus."

Hammy

"You did it."

This mirrors the SideQuestHQ experience:

Discover
→ Continue
→ Focus
→ Celebrate

---

# Mascot Usage Rules

## Do

- Use Kip everywhere as the primary companion.
- Use supporting mascots to reinforce emotional moments.
- Keep visual language warm and approachable.
- Emphasize curiosity, momentum, and continuity.
- Make learning feel human.

## Don't

- Treat supporting mascots as separate product brands.
- Create conflicting personalities.
- Use mascots to pressure users.
- Shame inactivity or broken streaks.
- Turn the characters into productivity coaches.

---

# Why Mascots Exist

SideQuestHQ is not selling content.

It is helping people continue.

The hardest part of self-directed learning is not understanding.

It is returning.

The mascots exist to make that return feel welcoming.

They transform the experience from:

"Manage your learning."

into

"Continue your journey."

Kip guides.

NovaPaw sparks curiosity.

Pando helps focus.

Hammy celebrates progress.

Together they support the core mission of SideQuestHQ:

**Helping learners finish what they start.**
