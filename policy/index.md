# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** June 2026

## Introduction

Welcome to SideQuestHQ ("SideQuestHQ", "we", "our", or "us").

SideQuestHQ helps learners organize, structure, and complete long-form learning content through personalized learning paths, adaptive pacing, and optional community-based cohorts.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we protect it, and the choices available to you regarding your information.

By using SideQuestHQ, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy.

---

# Information We Collect

## Information You Provide

When you create or use a SideQuestHQ account, you may provide:

* Name or display name
* Email address
* Profile photo
* Account credentials
* Learning interests and goals
* Notes and reflections
* Cohort participation information
* Messages, comments, or content shared within the platform
* Learning resources you submit (including URLs, playlists, videos, articles, and other content sources)

## Information Generated Through Your Use

While using SideQuestHQ, we may collect:

* Learning progress
* Completion history
* Session activity
* Cohort participation data
* Time spent learning
* Streaks and milestones
* Saved content
* Preferences and settings
* Feedback submissions

## Device and Technical Information

We may automatically collect:

* Device type
* Operating system version
* App version
* Language settings
* Time zone
* IP address
* Crash reports
* Diagnostic information
* Usage analytics

# AI and Content Processing

SideQuestHQ uses artificial intelligence and automated systems to analyze learning resources submitted by users.

This may include:

* Videos
* Playlists
* Articles
* Web pages
* Learning materials
* User-provided content

The purpose of this processing is to:

* Identify learning structure
* Generate lesson breakdowns
* Create learning paths
* Personalize pacing
* Recommend next learning steps
* Improve continuity and learning progression

Automated recommendations are intended to assist users and do not replace user choice. Users remain in control of their learning goals and decisions.
