### Section 3 — Problem Understanding

**What problem are you solving?**

People who want to deeply learn new skills often struggle to stay consistent. High-quality learning content is abundant, but long playlists, courses, and resources rarely fit into busy schedules. As priorities shift, learners lose momentum, abandon topics, and accumulate unfinished learning goals they genuinely care about.

**Who experiences this problem?**

A motivated learner with many interests and engagements (education, work, hobbies, or personal projects) but limited daily time because other responsibilities take priority.

**Why does this matter?**

People don't fail because they lack motivation or content. They fail because learning competes with everyday responsibilities. As consistency breaks, weeks become months, and subjects they genuinely cared about remain permanently stuck in "I'll get to it later."

### Section 4 — AI Thinking

**Why is AI the right approach?**

AI can understand the structure, difficulty, and dependencies within scattered articles, videos, playlists long form learning content under a domain, then automatically personalize pacing and next steps for each learner. Unlike static schedules or reminders, it continuously adapts as a user's available time, progress, and priorities change.


**Describe your AI-powered solution**

Users provide playlists, videos, articles, or other learning resources along with their goals and available time. AI analyzes the content and transforms it into a structured learning path with personalized pacing and daily sessions. Users can keep the path private or make it public, allowing others to join the same learning journey, share progress, exchange notes, and stay accountable together.
