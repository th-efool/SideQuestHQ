# What the App Actually Does

You can put in unstructured content:

- playlists
    
- blogs
    
- articles
    
- scattered videos
    
- or even very long 19hr+ videos
    

The app will break them down, dynamically chunk and schedule them according to your priorities.

You can come back daily for a little while and eventually, after a few weeks, be done with what you were learning.

It kind of creates cohorts.

If a cohort is public, anyone else can join.

You can send each other daily nudges and reminders, share notes, and other things.

You also don't need to create your own.

You can just look up cohorts for the topic you want to learn that have:

- high completion rate
    
- high active users
    

and join them.
